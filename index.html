Data Exploration in SQL

Performed exploratory analysis using SQL to query electric vehicle population data provided by the Department of Licensing, Washington State, USA, gotten from data.gov. The dataset was about two main electric vehicles - the Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and the Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs). It consists of about 125,000 rows. Furthermore, the data was queried for data cleaning and analyzed for key insights such as the electric vehicle with the highest number of registrations, the top 10 manufacturers, the vehicle model with the highest electric range (miles) greater than 100 miles per electric charge, etc.
